Enabling people to access essential information around GBV free of charge and anonymously. This innovative tool requires only a feature phone, with no internet connectivity needed. A collaborative effort between HDI-Rwanda and GIZ-Rwanda. As of November 27 (2024), the chatbot has reached an impressive 343,475 unique users, up from 160,000 on October 1st, with 1,991,660 interactions recorded today compared to 1,170,000 interactions in October.
